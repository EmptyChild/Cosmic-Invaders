ABOUT

Some useful information

In Cosmic Invaders you play as a defender of Earth against aliens invaders. Try to make best of it and show what you got!

Requirements

  • Computer or notebook with 1280x720 screen resolution (or higher)
  • Keyboard
  • Your amazing gaming skills!

CONTROLS & INTERFACE

How to play

Controls

  • Use arrow keys , , , to move your ship
  • Use [Spacebar] to shoot

How to play

  • You start with 100 HP, each time you get hit by enemy, you take damage. Don't let enemies to destroy your ship!
  • Try to avoid enemy shoots and kill more enemies
  • You earn score points for each enemy kill. Make the best you can!
